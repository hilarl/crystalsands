MALDIVES

Amidst the warm Indian Ocean is a group of coral islands that has captured the attention of many tourists as one of the best dive spots in the world. The Maldives is more than just underwater beauty; it is a country with culture, tradition and history.

A country's way of lifestyle and living condition will be best experienced by taking a walk in their footsteps and travel the way they do. Lodging in guest houses in inhabited islands has become very common form of hospitality in the Maldives. This is called affordable tourism, where tourists not only gets to experience the truly inner Maldives.

MAAFUSHI

Maafushi is the capital island of Central North Province of South East Asian Archipelago The Republic of Maldives. Maafushi is located nearby Ibrahim Nasir International Airport and Male', the capital city.

Maafushi is a beautiful island, with group of uninhabited islands, sand banks and gardens of coral reefs. Maafushi Island is an easygoing, foot-in-the-sand experience destination, surrounded by a wealthy echo-system in the coral reefs and the seas around the islands are calm with warm temperatures making it world's best for water sports, snorkeling and diving.
